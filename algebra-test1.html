Grade 1 Test – Algebra & Algebraic Thinking

Pensamiento algebraico: patrones, igualdades y faltantes (en inglés)

1) Which number makes the sentence true? 3 + __ = 7

2) What comes next in the pattern? ★, ●, ★, ●, ★, __

3) Fill in the blank: 5 − __ = 2

4) Which symbol makes the sentence true? 6 __ 4

5) If □ = 8, what is □ − 3?

6) Rule: “Add 2.” If the first number is 1, what is the third number?

7) Which equation is correct?

8) Complete the number sentence: 10 = __ + 6

9) Which pattern is growing by 2?

10) If △ = 2 and ○ = 4, what is △ + ○?

11) Choose the equation that is balanced (true): 4 + 1 __ 2 + 3

12) Mia has 6 apples. She gives 2 to her friend. How many apples does she have now?
